My name is Michael Funk and for the past 15 years I have loved drawing. That's basically my entire life. I've always seen art as a fun way to connect with and understand other people. Whether that's through a fun and quirky way or a more serious tone, my intention is for my work to connect with others. And hopefully, they can enjoy that work for years to come. While there are many mediums an artist can use, I prefer to use the most common: a pencil. I also have done lots of work digitally because it makes the process go quicker and is a bit more practical. I find both mediums to be satisfying for different reasons, but overall I use them both to accomplish the same purpose. ​ This page is simply here to help you understand my goals as an artist. If you want to understand more about my creative process on satirical artwork, check out my thoughts below. Or take a look at my portfolio to see examples of my work!
